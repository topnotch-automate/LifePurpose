Most people treat mind renewal as something they need to earn or wait for. 
This video explains why that misunderstands both the nature of God and the nature of you.

We unpack two words — aseity and seity — and use them to resolve one of the most misread tensions in Christian thought: if God works in you, why must you work at all? 
The answer is not a compromise. It is a precise understanding of how the ONE expresses Himself through the MANY.

Three independent witnesses arrive at the same conclusion — a 19th-century metaphysical thinker, a 21st-century quantum physicist, and a 1st-century apostle. The convergence is the point.

## Key Points

- God works in you as the ground of your willing and acting
- You transform by mind renewal by recognizing yourself in God
- You are the site where God’s working is manifested
